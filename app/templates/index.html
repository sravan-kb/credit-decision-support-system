Understanding Risk Categories

P1

Very Low Risk

Strong credit history, healthy income, and minimal delinquency signals. Highly likely to be approved with favorable terms.

P2

Low Risk

Generally reliable credit behavior with minor irregularities. Approval is likely under standard terms.

P3

Medium Risk

Some signs of financial stress or inconsistent repayment history. Requires closer review before approval.

P4

High Risk

Significant delinquency history or unstable financial indicators. High likelihood of rejection or added scrutiny.
